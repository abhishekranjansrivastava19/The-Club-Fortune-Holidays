Our Privacy Policy

1 - Introduction

Welcome to The Club Fortune Holidays. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2 - Information We Collect

(a) - Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing address
  • Payment information (credit card details, billing address)

(b) - Non-Personal Information:

  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time and date of visit

3 - How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and managing your travel bookings
  • Sending booking confirmations and travel updates
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Improving our website and services
  • Sending promotional emails (you can opt-out at any time)
  • Conducting market research and analysis

4 - Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted third parties to provide you with services, including:

  • Payment processors
  • Travel service providers (airlines, hotels, car rental companies)
  • Marketing and advertising partners

These third parties are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5 - Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information. These include:

  • Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt your information during transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to your personal information

6 - Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

7 - Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request the deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

8 - Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9 - Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
