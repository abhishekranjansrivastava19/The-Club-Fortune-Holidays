Rishikesh

Yoga and Adventure: The Dual Appeal of Rishikesh

Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, is renowned as the "Yoga Capital of the World." It attracts spiritual seekers from around the globe to its numerous ashrams and yoga retreats. For adventure enthusiasts, Rishikesh offers thrilling white-water rafting on the Ganges River, trekking in the surrounding hills, and camping by the riverside. The iconic Laxman Jhula and the tranquil ghats provide perfect spots for reflection and relaxation.
